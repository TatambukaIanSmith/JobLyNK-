## 🎨 Design Principles

### 1. Consistency
- Matches application form design
- Same color scheme throughout
- Consistent spacing and padding

### 2. Hierarchy
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Important data stands out
- Easy to scan and read

### 3. Professionalism
- Clean and organized
- Professional color scheme
- Business-ready appearance

### 4. Branding
- JOB-lyNK logo prominent
- Brand colors used consistently
- Tagline included

### 5. Readability
- Clear typography
- Good contrast
- Adequate spacing
